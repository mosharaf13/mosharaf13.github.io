ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Md Mosharaf Hossan, but you’ll mostly meet me as mosharaf13 on the internet. I’m a masters student at Idaho State University

I am a Software Engineer and researcher with over five years of experience building scalable solutions for startups and enterprise environments. I have led teams to develop telecom automation tools and multi-tenant SaaS solutions, and I have contributed to large-scale marketplace applications.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science (GPA 3.95) at Idaho State University, where I'm developing a biofeedback adaptive VR game to help people with mysophobia based OCD manage their symptoms.
